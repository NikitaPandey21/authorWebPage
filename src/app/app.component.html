About Nikita

Nikita grew up in Pune, India, which has a rich historical past in the state of Maharashtra. She is a software engineer and firmly believes in the power of storytelling to transport readers to new worlds, challenge their perspectives, and evoke a range of emotions.

Nikita debuted in the world of literature with the fiction novel “The Day I Live For” which is a romantic mystery. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for creating authentic dialogue, she invites readers on an unforgettable literary journey.

Apart from being a software engineer and an author, Nikita holds a great interest in music. She is also a classical singer.

“The Day I Live For” is Nikita’s first foray into the world of published fiction, and she is excited to share this story with readers everywhere. She looks forward to connecting with the literary community and embarking on this new chapter of her writing career.

“ The Day I Live For ”

Hi, this is Aman, love was the most unexpected thing that happened to me when I met Radhika. Soon we were going to get married and then on the day of our engagement all dreams broke at once.

She was poisoned on the day of our engagement.

Was it my love for her that tried to kill her? Or something else?

Sometimes I feel if I wasn’t part of her life, she would have been at a better place...
